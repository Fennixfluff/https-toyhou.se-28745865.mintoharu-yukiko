About Yukiko

First off the name, Mintoharu is his last name. Minto means mint, and haru means spring. His first name is Yukiko. Yuki means Happiness or good fortune, and ko is a name suffix than means child. the suffix ko makes the name more feminine, however this his parents named him it for his gentle nature. He generally only goes by Yuki though.

Yukiko lives in a small cottage in the outskirts of town, where he tends to his gardens. He is well known for his mint, as it has an unusually rich fragrance coupled with its rich flavor. Yukiko is fond of the moss he grows, as its able to stay its vibrant green and spongy texture for weeks after being harvested--even longer if one sticks to plant care schedule and waters the moss on occasion. Every year during the festival he sets up his stand of the various herbs he grows--which sell out every time. However he also tries endlessly to share his love of moss with others, even going as far as offering free moss with every purchase. Only a few people in town share his enthusiasm. Yukiko is a kind hearted soul and often spends time with the kids in the town.

Trivia
